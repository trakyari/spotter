Job Interviews

Uttermost Importance.

Job Interviews are a very important part of getting a job, an interview is one of the easiest ways to determine if a jobseeker is the right person. Personal interviews are used by all employers and companies for selecting their staff. An interview is one of the most important steps in the staff selection procedure. Interviews prove important because they connects both the employers as well as jobseekers. Interviews are usually the deciding factor and what one does or how one prepares for the interview are important.

Below are provided the general do's and dont's regarding an in person interview - note: these tips may not apply to all because of varying situations and should only be taken as a guideline.

The Do's
  • Always arrive early for an interview
  • Dress for success, ie. suit
  • Shake the employerâ€™s hand and thank them for their time
  • Research your employer and the job
The Dont's
  • Dress sloppily
  • Fail to look at the employer during the interview
  • Focus on the money aspect of the job
  • Use slang during the interview
